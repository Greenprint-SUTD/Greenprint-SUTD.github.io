About Us

SUTD Greenprint is an environmental club. We strive to promote a sustainable lifestyle and generate awareness on the environmental issues the world faces today. We believe that everyone can do their part, big or small to curtail and reverse the damage done on the environment. Something as simple as cutting down on the usage of one-time use plastic, such as straws, can go a long way in alleviating the plight of our Planet Earth.
